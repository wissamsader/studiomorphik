About

STUDIOMORPHIK is a creative team crafting AI-driven video and image solutions for brands, products, and campaigns. Founded by photographer and visual artist Wissam Sader and editor and motion designer Teddy Tawil, and working between Europe and the Middle East, we produce everything from custom characters, landscapes, and cinematic transitions to product, food, and automotive shots, beauty, VFX, and hero films — blending true photorealism with the surreal and imaginative. Whether you need a full campaign or a focused VFX solution on any budget, we mix the right styles, models, and techniques to bring your vision to life. We've created work with brands and media companies including Vice Media and KitKat, among many others.
