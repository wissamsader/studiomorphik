momzu

A voice AI that lives on your Mac. Hold a key, say what you want, let go. It talks back, drives your real browser, reads your screen, remembers you, plays your music, keeps your calendar, and leaves finished work on your disk.

Your voice never leaves the machine. Speech recognition runs locally, replies are spoken locally, and the cloud only ever sees text, only if you choose a cloud model.

MORPH

An image and video effects studio. Drop in a photo or a video, then turn it into something else: halftone, ordered dither, ascii, contour, glitch, riso. Every dial moves while you watch.

It renders on your own machine, not on a farm. No account, no uploads, no queue. The engine is ours, written from scratch.
