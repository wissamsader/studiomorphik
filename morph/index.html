what it does
01

Drop anything in.

A photo, a video — straight onto the canvas. The effect renders live, at full quality, while you drag the dials. No import step, no timeline, no waiting. It reacts like an instrument, not like software.

02

Stack a look.

Effects layer on top of each other into looks — halftone under film grain under bloom. Save the ones you love, recall them with one click, tune every slider on the way.

03

It hears the music.

Play a track inside MORPH and the image starts moving to it — kick drives the dots, highs shimmer the grain. Your own files, a built-in player, a reactivity panel to decide exactly what the sound touches.

sim · sound
reactive
04

Record the show.

One button captures exactly what you see — frame-exact 30fps, music baked into the file. No screen-recording permission dance, no interface in the shot, no dropped frames. Strobe mode for the brave.

05

Export without uploading.

Renders happen on your Mac, not on a farm. Pick a duration, press export, and an MP4 lands on your disk — no queue, no watermark, no account asking to be created.

06

3D Lab & Motion Lab.

Two more rooms in the same studio. In the 3D Lab, type becomes sculpture you can light and spin. In the Motion Lab, still images learn to move — drift, ripple, breathe.

Nothing leaves your Mac.
